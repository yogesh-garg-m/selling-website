Selling Guidelines

Eligibility to Sell +

You must be at least 18 years old to post items for sale. You must have a valid InstiOLX account to list items.

Listing Items +

Provide accurate and detailed descriptions of the items you are selling. Clearly mention the price, condition, and any defects or issues with the item. Upload high-quality images of the actual item being sold.

Prohibited Items +

The sale of illegal, stolen, or counterfeit items is strictly prohibited. Items that are offensive, discriminatory, or violate any laws are not allowed.

Pricing +

Set fair and reasonable prices for your items. InstiOLX does not regulate prices but reserves the right to remove listings with excessive pricing.

Communication +

Respond promptly to inquiries and communicate politely with potential buyers. Do not share personal information (e.g., bank details) until you are sure of the buyer's intent.

Transactions +

Prefer meeting in public places for exchanging items and payments. Ensure that the payment is received in full before handing over the item. InstiOLX is not responsible for any disputes arising from transactions between buyers and sellers.

Post-Sale Responsibilities +

Provide after-sale support if applicable (e.g., transfer of warranties). Address any legitimate complaints from buyers regarding the item sold.

Reporting Issues +

Report any suspicious activity or violations of these guidelines to InstiOLX immediately. Use the reporting tools provided on the website to flag inappropriate listings or behavior.
